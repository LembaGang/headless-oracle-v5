# Headless Oracle Legal Playbook - Mandatory Clauses

1. **Data Source Disclaimer:** We must explicitly state that we do not generate market data. We only report the status provided by third-party exchanges (NYSE, LSE, etc.). We are not liable if the underlying exchange reports incorrect hours or halts.
2. **Signature Scope:** We must clarify that the Ed25519 signature only guarantees that the payload was generated by our servers at the timestamped moment. It does not guarantee the underlying financial accuracy of the data itself.
3. **Limitation of Liability:** Our maximum total liability for any catastrophic failure, bad debt, or missed liquidation caused by our API must be strictly capped at the amount the user paid for their subscription in the last 12 months.
4. **No Financial Advice:** The API provides raw binary status data (OPEN/CLOSED) and is not financial, trading, or investment advice.
